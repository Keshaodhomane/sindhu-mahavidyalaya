WELCOME TO SINDHU MAHAVIDYALAYA

Dada Ramchand Bakhru Sindhu Mahavidyalaya ( formerly known as Sindhu Mahavidyalaya ) is a 44 year old premier institution of Nagpur imparting quality education to students in the faculties of Science & Commerce. It is run by Sindhi Hindi Vidya Samiti which comprises of a team of social workers committed to the mission of propogating education. The college was founded on 1st July 1971. The college is reaccredited by NAAC in Grade B with CGPA 2.62 The courses run by the college are B.Sc.(Chemistry, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Biochemistry, Electronics, Physics, Maths, Botany, Zoology, Computer Science), M.Sc.(Biotech.) in science faculty. In commerce faculty B.Com, B.B.A. and M.Com. The institute also has junior college(+2 level) in Science, Commerce and MCVC. The college has UGC network resource centre, Equal Oppurtinuity Cell, NCC unit, NSS and Adult Education, Infilibnet, library facilities and computer centre with internet.
